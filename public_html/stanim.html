Resident Hub

Welcome to the Stanford Internal Medicine Resident Hub. This page contains links to common resources used throughout residency and work at Stanford, the VA, and Santa Clara Valley. It is maintained by Alex Goodell - for suggestions reach out over text/whatsapp/email. This site is powered by http://start.me; you can sign up for an account if youd like to make a custom version (remember to share publicly so you can access on different computers).
